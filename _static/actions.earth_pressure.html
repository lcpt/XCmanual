EarthPressure¶

earth_pressure.py: earth pressure over finite element models.

class actions.earth_pressure.EarthPressure.EarthPressure(K, zTerrain, gammaTerrain, zWater, gammaWater, vDir)¶

Parameters to define a load of type earth pressure Attributes:

K: Coefficient of pressure zTerrain:global Z coordinate of ground level gammaTerrain: weight density of soil zWater: global Z coordinate of groundwater level

(if zGroundwater<minimum z of model => there is no groundwater)

gammaWater: weight density of water vDir: unit vector defining pressures direction
